HARN Application

HARN is a smart office building in Bangkok, Thailand. The HARN Application is used to control the building's systems such as lighting, temperature, and meeting room booking. Contacting the service team and general building info can also be used with the application.

Design

The colour palette of the application is based on the company's identity, which is green and red. The interface was created to be clear and simple to grasp, making it suited for all users.
